Our Guiding Principles

Relationships & Joy

Cultivate a diverse learning environment that is engaging, energetic, and filled with moments of celebration. Prioritize strong relationships that foster trust, active listening, open communication, and developing the CORES.

Student-Centered & Personalized

Empowers student ownership through inquiry, project-based, and career-based learning that values diverse identities and perspectives. Uses flexible pacing, personalized support, and differentiation to meet individual learning needs.

Authentic & Purposeful

Connects content learning and the CORES to real-world applications, careers, and interdisciplinary experiences. Utilizes meaningful, performance and growth-based assessments beyond traditional tests.

Community & ʻĀina-Based

Rooted in Hawai‘i, we honor and integrate local culture and history. Fosters community partnerships, field studies, and service learning for purposeful engagement.

Blended & Innovative

Seamlessly combines in-person, virtual, and independent learning for engagement and flexibility. Necessitates creative problem-solving, experimentation, iteration, and the use of technology.
